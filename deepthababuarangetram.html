Deeptha Babu's Arangetram

Celebrate the artistic journey of Deeptha Babu as she takes the stage for her Arangetram, guided by her esteemed Guru, Smt. Vidyalakshmi Vinod. This landmark performance is a testament to years of unwavering dedication, disciplined training, and a profound connection to the rich tradition of Bharatanatyam.

Date: September 13th @ 2.30 PM

Venue: Renton IKEA Performing Arts Center, Renton, WA
